Fol Epi Organic Bakery

Location Address: 398 Habour Road #101, Victoria, BC V9A 0B7

Fol Epi Organic Bakery is an organic artisan bakery located near the upper habour in Victoria, B.C.. Established in 2009, this bakery is keeping the tradition of local, sustainable, and traditional baking techniques at the forefront of their operation. Traditional practices include organic grains milled on site, utilizing wood-fired brick overs, and offering both traditional and modern selection. Items available include breads, cookies, macarons, tarts, and many other baked treats that anyways sweet tooth may desire.
